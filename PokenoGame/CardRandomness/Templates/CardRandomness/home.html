Never heard of Pokeno? That's ok!

Pokeno is basically poker meets bingo! You start by choosing 25 playing cards from a deck of cards, placing them in a 5x5 grid. After everyone has chosen their cards, the game begins with an automated dealer revealing cards slowly one by one.

Click on your board to mark a card as being revealed! Once you have five cards in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally), that is your "hand". After everyone has a "hand", whosoever has the highest poker "hand" wins!
